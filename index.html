About this dude

who is he again?
Kenta David is a Queens-based Illustrator & Animator. He Founded Kester Team at a young age in 2005 and reformed it in 2013. The First Original Series were Spiky-Hair,The Odd Ones Out, Jam Slugs, Bepo! The Smol Bean and the first Monthly Shonen Manga, Dreamcasters. Kenta is currently remaking his first Weekly Shonen manga, Hibari's Excellent Adventure.
